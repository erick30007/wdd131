Explore Local Wonders

Site Name

Explore Local Wonders

The site name was selected because it reflects the main purpose of the website, which is to guide users in discovering local attractions and experiences in their region.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a local guide to help users find and explore key attractions, dining spots, and events in their city or region.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: "What are the must-see landmarks in the city?"

Scenario 2: "Where can I find recommendations for family-friendly restaurants?"

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color (#3498db) - Used for headers, navigation, and accent elements.
  • Secondary Color (#f39c12) - Used for buttons, highlights, and links.

Typography

Primary Font: Roboto - Used for body text to ensure readability.

Secondary Font: Lora - Used for headings to add style and personality.

Wireframe

The homepage will include a simple header. Below that, Sections will be stacked vertically, including highlights for local landmarks, restaurants, and events. A footer with contact information and social links will appear at the bottom.

The header will span the top with navigation links visible. An image will showcase key attractions, followed by grid sections highlighting landmarks, dining, and events. The footer will include additional links and social media icons.
